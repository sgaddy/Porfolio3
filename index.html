About Me

My name is Piggly. I am a domesticated pig. I eat both plants and meat. Sometimes, I even eat metal. Just kidding. I am highly sociable and intelligent. I can be trained to perform numerous tasks and tricks.

To the right is a pictures of me when I was a baby pig. I am so adorable. There are about 1 billion of us alive at any time. We are one of the most numerous large mammals on the planet. Our ancestor the wild boar are also one of the most numerous and widespread large mammals in nature.

Below is a picture of me with my best friend Fred. As an adult pig, I can weigh between 50 and 350 kg (110 and 770 lb). I enjoy seeking the company of other pigs and I often huddle with other pigs to maintain physical contact. My favor snack is corn on the cob. My favor color is pink and my favor movie star is BABE
